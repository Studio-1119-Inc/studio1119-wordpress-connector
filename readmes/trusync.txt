== Description ==

{{APP_PLUGIN_NAME}} connects your WooCommerce store to the {{APP_MENU_TITLE}} cloud service, which automatically posts your orders and inventory to QuickBooks Online so your accounting records stay in sync without manual data entry.

Once you connect your store, {{APP_MENU_TITLE}} keeps WooCommerce and QuickBooks Online aligned:

* **Order sync** — each WooCommerce order is posted to QuickBooks Online as the matching sales document, in real time via WooCommerce webhooks.
* **Inventory sync** — stock changes flow between QuickBooks Online and WooCommerce so quantities match in both systems.
* **Payment mapping** — order payment methods are mapped to the correct QuickBooks Online deposit accounts.
* **Sales tax mapping** — order tax lines are mapped to your QuickBooks Online tax jurisdictions.
* **Product matching** — your WooCommerce products are matched to existing QuickBooks Online items, with historical order backfill available.

= External service =

{{APP_PLUGIN_NAME}} is the WooCommerce connector for the {{APP_MENU_TITLE}} service, and a {{APP_MENU_TITLE}} account is required. All order processing, QuickBooks Online communication, billing, and account management happen on the {{APP_MENU_TITLE}} service. The plugin securely links your store to that service over the WooCommerce REST API and provides single sign-on to the {{APP_MENU_TITLE}} dashboard. No accounting data is stored in WordPress.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin zip through **Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin**, or install from the WordPress.org directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the **Plugins** menu in WordPress.
3. Click the **{{APP_MENU_TITLE}}** menu item in the admin sidebar.
4. Click **Connect** to authorize the connection to your {{APP_MENU_TITLE}} account and start syncing.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need a QuickBooks Online account? =

Yes. {{APP_MENU_TITLE}} synchronizes your WooCommerce store with QuickBooks Online, so an active QuickBooks Online account is required.

= Do I need a {{APP_MENU_TITLE}} account? =

Yes. The plugin connects your store to the {{APP_MENU_TITLE}} service, which performs the synchronization. You can create an account during the connection step.

= Where does the synchronization happen? =

Order and inventory processing happens on the {{APP_MENU_TITLE}} service. The plugin connects your store to that service and does not store accounting data in your WordPress database.

= What happens when I uninstall the plugin? =

The plugin removes its own options, the WooCommerce webhooks it registered, and the WooCommerce API keys created during connection. Your WooCommerce orders and products are never modified or deleted.
